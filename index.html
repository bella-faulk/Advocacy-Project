About My Topic

To start learning more about my topic, the issue of climate justice begins with the disproportionate effects of climate change on people who have had the least cause of it, such as overall emissions or pollution. Although climate change may feel like a newer issue in today's age, in the past there have been movements and actions towards preventing it, with a long and interesting history.

Climate justice also addresses how countries who hold the least amount of responsibility in causing climate change are already bearing the gravest consequences, and how we can mitigate these harmful effects. In order to protect our future from further harm and to achieve climate justice, it is essential for us to take action now.
